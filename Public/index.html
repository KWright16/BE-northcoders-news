NC News

GET /api/topics
Serves up all the topics

GET /api/topics/:topic_slug/articles
Serves up all the articles for a certain topic

POST /api/topics/:topic_slug/articles
Adds a new article to a topic.

GET /api/articles
Serves up all the articles

GET /api/articles/:article_id
Serves up an individual article

GET /api/articles/:article_id/comments
Serves up all the comments for a individual article

POST /api/articles/:article_id/comments
Adds a new comment to an article.

PUT /api/articles/:article_id
Increments or Decrements the votes of an article by one. This route requires a vote query of 'up' or 'down'

PUT /api/comments/:comment_id
Increments or Decrements the votes of a comment by one. This route requires a vote query of 'up' or 'down'

DELETE /api/comments/:comment_id
Deletes a comment

GET /api/users/:username
Serves up all the users
